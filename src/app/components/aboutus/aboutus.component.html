About Us

Cognizant

Our History

Founded in 1994, Cognizant has grown to become a global leader in business and technology services. With a commitment to innovation and client success, Cognizant empowers organizations to transform their operations and embrace the digital future.

Our Vision for Knowledge

Cognizant believes in the power of knowledge sharing and continuous learning. Our vision is to foster a culture where expertise is accessible, collaboration is encouraged, and every team member is empowered to grow and contribute to organizational excellence.

NRG

Our History

NRG Energy, founded in 1989, is a leading integrated power company in the U.S., delivering innovative solutions and sustainable energy to millions of customers. NRG is dedicated to driving progress in the energy sector through technology and customer-centric strategies.

Our Vision for Knowledge

At NRG, we value knowledge as a key driver of innovation and operational excellence. Our vision is to create an environment where knowledge is shared openly, enabling our teams to deliver smarter, cleaner, and more reliable energy solutions for the future.
